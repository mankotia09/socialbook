Research Assistant (RA) Support Group

Graduate Research Assistants can find support, share resources, and build community at the RA Support Group, led by the College of Graduate Studies and graduate faculty members. This group is ideal for graduate students who wish to discuss their struggles and successes during the research process. Any graduate student or postdoc who is currently planning or conducting research is encouraged to join. Baked goods will be provided.

Exploring the Information Landscape | King Arthur: Real Person, Real Uncertainty

When Monday, March 20, 2023, 12 to 1pm University Location First Floor Classroom (120) - Library - Main Campus Details The figure of King Arthur is presented throughout history in a variety of ways. We will briefly review popular films, literature and historical sites to familiarize ourselves with the character of King Arthur. After walking through these resources, we will focus on a few aspects of King Arthur's legend while discussing their presence and impact in modern times. In today’s workshop, you’ll expand your knowledge of how you think about a research topic, while making interdisciplinary connections between seemingly simple stories.
